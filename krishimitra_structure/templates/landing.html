Indian Farming Evolution

2000 BCE

Indus Valley Civilization: Early architectural irrigation channels and systematic crop-rotation systems across regional belts.

1960s

The Green Revolution: Introduction of High-Yielding Variety (HYV) seeds, modern machinery installations, and fertilizer paradigms.

Present Day

Digital Farming Era: Precision algorithms, automated sensor data streaming, and ML prediction platforms driving micro-level field analytics.
